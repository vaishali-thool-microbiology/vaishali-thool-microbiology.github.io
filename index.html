About Me

I’m Vaishali Thool. Presently I’m serving as an Associate Professor of Microbiology in Sardar Patel Mahavidyalaya, Chandrapur, Maharashtra, India. I have a special interest in the field of Medical Microbiology and is a Research Guide in Gondwana University, Gadchiroli. I have at present Twenty five National and International research papers to my credit and have authored Fourteen books for graduate students in the field of Microbiology. Demonstrated a continued commitment in teaching students and advising students about their skills, career opportunities, the pursuit of advanced degrees, etc; from last twenty-five years. My other contributions to the institution are through various committees like IQAC, Career Guidance Committee, and serving as Head of Alumni Committee. Also as a part of social connect delivered various guest lectures on social and environmental issues.
